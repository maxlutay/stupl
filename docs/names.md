Names

 * not contain
    * ' ', ''', '"'
    * '-', '+', '/', '*', '^'  
    * '<', '>'
    * '='
    * ':', '.', ',', ';'
    * '!', '?'
    * '#'
    * '%', '\'
    * '@'
    * '$'
    * '&' and '|'
    * '{' and '}' 
    * '(' and ')'
    * '[' and ']'


 * first letter
    * not '0-9'
    * 'A-Z' or 'a-z'


 * 2nd and next letters
    * optional
    * any UNICODE


 *examples
    * correct
        * `bob`, `john`, `s_0125a`, `A4ter`, `i`
    * incorrect 
        * `_bob`, `1stjohn`, `-s_0125a`,`4Ater`, `1`
